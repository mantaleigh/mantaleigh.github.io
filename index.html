Featured Project

Minesweeper with Leapmotion

Changing up the way you interact with one of your favorite classic games

Finalist

The goal of this project was for the team of four of us to learn an entirely new API during the span of the hackathon. We built a (not pretty, but functional) minesweeper game and then programmed different hand motions used to play. Moving your right hand in a circle reveals what's behind a square and pushing your left index finger towards the screen uses a disarm kit on a mine, allowing you to safely uncover the square. We also included images of Drumpf as the mines for the lols.

ReYAKtions

Project with a team at Camp WHACK, Spring 2016.
Winner - 2nd Place
Many prospective students arrive on campuses and analyze Yik Yak feeds. This can be time consuming and imprecise. This web app allows students who are searching for colleges to find out exactly how the students are feeling at that school based on sentiment analysis of the campus' recent yaks and a wordcloud associated with those yaks. This was my second adventure in backend development, and my first exposure to Flask.

Project with a team at Hack@Brown, Spring 2016.
Winner - Beginner Hack
The beautiful result of 4 women deciding that it was time to have a little fun at a hackathon and push ourselves out of our comfort zones.
